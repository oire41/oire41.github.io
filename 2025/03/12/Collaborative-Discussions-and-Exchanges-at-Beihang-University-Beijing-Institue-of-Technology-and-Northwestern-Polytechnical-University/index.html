Collaborative Discussions and Exchanges at Beihang University, Beijing Institute of Technology(BIT), and Northwestern Polytechnical University(NWPU)

From March 6 to March 10, the delegation of the Office of Institution Research and Evaluation, led by the Dean Dan WU, visited Beihang University, Beijing Institute of Technology, and Northwestern Polytechnical University.

We engaged in in-depth discussions on academic connotation expansion profiling evaluation, academic research direction evaluation for faculty members, and other related topics with these three universities. Also, we exchanged ideas on how to further improve the evaluation index system, optimize evaluation methods, and eventually enhance the application of evaluation results.

As we universities serving the nation’s major strategic needs in defense science and technology, NUDT, Beihang University, Beijing Institute of Technology, and Northwestern Polytechnical University have established a strong collaborative mechanism in evaluation. Through these discussions, we firmly believe that we share broad prospects for cooperation in many areas, such as discipline construction, talents cultivation, and of course the scientific research. The exchange visit has further strengthened our collaborative efforts.

Taking this opportunity, Beihang University, Beijing Institute of Technology, and Northwestern Polytechnical University expressed their commitment to deepening exchanges and cooperation with us, NUDT. We aim to explore effective approaches to improving governance systems and management mechanisms, contributing more to serving the nation’s major strategic needs and building world-class universities.
